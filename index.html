Understanding the Badge

  • Red: High concern ingredients
  • Yellow: Moderate concern ingredients
  • Green: No concerning ingredients
  • Gray: No ingredient data available

The number indicates how many concerning ingredients were found in the product.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

The Toxin Filter Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Product Information

  • Product ingredients and their composition
  • Product identifiers from Instacart
  • Product URLs and image URLs
  • Price information displayed on Instacart

User Account Information

  • Email address (for account creation and management)
  • Subscription status and history
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

How We Use Your Information

Product Analysis

  • To analyze ingredients for potential health concerns
  • To provide visual feedback about ingredient safety
  • To maintain a database of product ingredients for faster future analysis
  • To improve the accuracy of our ingredient analysis system

Account Management

  • To manage your subscription status
  • To process payments through our payment processor, Stripe
  • To communicate important updates about your account or the service

Data Storage and Security

We use the following methods to store and protect your data:

  • Secure database storage through Supabase for product and user data
  • Local browser caching for improved performance
  • Encrypted transmission of all data using HTTPS
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Stripe - for processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Supabase - for secure database hosting

Local Storage

The extension uses local browser storage to:

  • Cache product information for faster performance
  • Store user preferences and settings
  • Maintain authentication state

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (note: this may limit functionality)

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you cancel your subscription, we will retain your data for a reasonable period to comply with legal obligations and for backup purposes.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
